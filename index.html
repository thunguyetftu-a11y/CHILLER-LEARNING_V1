📚 Learning Rules & Scoring

\n \n
\n
\n
\n

⏱️ How It Works

\n
    \n
  • Each question has 10 seconds to answer
    • \n
  • Questions are randomly shuffled each time you play
    • \n
  • Answer before time runs out to score points
    • \n
  • After 10 seconds, automatically moves to next question
    • \n
  • No turning back to previous questions
    • \n
\n
\n\n
\n

⭐ Scoring System

\n
\n
\n Answered in 1 sec:\n 10 + 5 = 15 points\n
\n
\n Answered in 2 sec:\n 10 + 4 = 14 points\n
\n
\n Answered in 3 sec:\n 10 + 3 = 13 points\n
\n
\n Answered in 4 sec:\n 10 + 2 = 12 points\n
\n
\n Answered in 5 sec:\n 10 + 1 = 11 points\n
\n
\n Answered in 6-10 sec:\n 10 + 0 = 10 points\n
\n
\n Wrong answer:\n 0 points\n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n

🎯 Points Breakdown

\n
    \n
  • Standard Points (Blue): 10 points for each correct answer
    • \n
  • Bonus Points (Orange): Extra points for answering quickly (within 5 seconds)
    • \n
  • Maximum per question: 15 points (10 standard + 5 bonus)
    • \n
